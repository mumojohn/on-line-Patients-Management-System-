Welcome to Online Patient Management System

An Online Patient Management System is a tool that helps manage administrative tasks and store patient records. It aims at improving practice efficiency, delivering timely care, and improving patient experience and outcomes. It typically performs the following functions: automates appointment scheduling, provides e-forms or intake forms for patients to fill before admission, records interactions, manages referrals, allows payments, maintains electronic records of your patients and their medical history, and makes it easy for patients to access test results, prescriptions, etc. through patient portals.
